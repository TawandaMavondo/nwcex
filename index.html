About the Exchange

A national platform for the people who shape social care

A national exchange for providers, commissioners and sector leaders.

Though North-West rooted, NWCEX convenes leaders from across the UK to share best practice, confront the sector's hardest challenges, and build the relationships that improve outcomes for service users. We are a premium forum, a growing executive network, and the place where the future of care is discussed candidly.
